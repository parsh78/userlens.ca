THE GIST SECTION - Travel Case Study
Generated from: US travelers going to Cancun case study

========================================
PROBLEM (Red dot):
========================================
A travel platform had solid acquisition and first booking numbers, but repeat booking rates were below target. Leadership wanted to improve retention without adding more features — but didn't have evidence for what was actually driving customers to return or drift to competitors.

========================================
APPROACH (Blue dot):
========================================
Ran a full customer journey engagement — longitudinal interviews at three points (before booking, immediately after, weeks later), mapped loyalty and loss moments across the entire experience, and delivered a prioritized CX strategy framework showing which moments mattered most to retention.

========================================
OUTCOME (Green dot):
========================================
Three existing product touchpoints redesigned and two new ones introduced specifically around loyalty drivers. Result: 17% lift in engagement and return booking rates, 30% reduction in unsubscribe rates, and a reusable customer journey map for evaluating future initiatives.

========================================

========================================

Key moments identified:
1. Post-booking personalized messages → Built loyalty
